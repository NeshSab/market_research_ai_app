# Sector Explainers

## Technology

Composition: software; semiconductors; hardware; IT services. Drivers:
earnings growth; real yields; capex cycles; USD; innovation cadence.
Macro Sensitivity: pro-cyclical; strong in disinflationary expansion;
rate-sensitive. KPIs: revenue growth; FCF margins; R&D; semi
inventories; forward P/E. Notes: semis cyclical; software duration;
avoid overstating rates.

## Financials

Composition: banks; insurance; capital markets; payments. Drivers: curve
slope; credit cycle; deposit flows; regulation. Macro Sensitivity:
prefer steepening; weak in recession; insurers like long-end rates.
KPIs: NIM; loan growth; charge-offs; CET1; deposit betas. Notes: curve
shape \> level; credit quality key.

## Energy

Composition: integrateds; E&P; services; midstream. Drivers: oil/gas
prices; supply/demand; OPEC; USD. Macro Sensitivity:
inflationary/geopolitical regimes. KPIs: realized prices; production;
capex; FCF. Notes: oil vs gas differ.

## Industrials

Composition: machinery; aerospace/defense; transportation. Drivers:
PMIs; capex; freight; defense budgets. Macro Sensitivity: global growth
beta; early-cycle strong. KPIs: book-to-bill; backlog; utilization.
Notes: defense decouples.

## Consumer Discretionary

Composition: retail; e-commerce; autos; travel; luxury. Drivers: labor;
wages; credit; fuel. Macro Sensitivity: pro-cyclical. KPIs: same-store
sales; inventory; ASPs; auto units. Notes: luxury ≠ mass market.

## Consumer Staples

Composition: food/bev; household; personal care; tobacco. Drivers:
pricing; input costs; FX; volumes. Macro Sensitivity: defensive. KPIs:
price/volume mix; margins. Notes: pass-through varies.

## Utilities

Composition: electric; gas; water; IPPs. Drivers: rates; regulated ROE;
fuel mix; capex. Macro Sensitivity: defensive; rate-sensitive. KPIs:
rate base; ROE; leverage. Notes: IPPs differ.

## Materials

Composition: mining; chemicals; construction; packaging. Drivers:
commodities; PMIs; China; spreads. Macro Sensitivity: early-cycle;
inflation-linked. KPIs: realized prices; cost curves. Notes: China
leverage high.

## Real Estate

Composition: retail; industrial; office; resi; data centers. Drivers:
rates; leasing; supply; capital availability. Macro Sensitivity:
rate-sensitive. KPIs: FFO; occupancy; NOI; cap rates. Notes: subsector
dispersion high.

## Communication Services

Composition: internet; media; telecom. Drivers: ads; subs; ARPU; churn;
content spend. Macro Sensitivity: mixed. KPIs: ad growth; churn; ARPU.
Notes: telecom ≠ media.

## Health Care

Composition: pharma; biotech; devices; providers; managed care. Drivers:
pipelines; procedures; reimbursement; demographics. Macro Sensitivity:
defensive. KPIs: R&D productivity; patent cliffs; MLR. Notes: subsector
risks vary.
